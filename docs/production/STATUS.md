Production covers everything around the game itself: audio, music, marketing, the Steam page, the free demo, the post-launch update plan, eventually mod support and multiplayer.

## Decisions captured (high-level)

**V1 launches on Steam as Early Access at $20.** Pricing increases as content lands toward 1.0.

**Free demo planned.** Specific scope TBD. Should demonstrate the architectural advantages without requiring purchase.

**Post-launch update plan.** Working framing: v1 is complete-but-evolving with a clearly-stated roadmap. Updates are additions, not fixes for missing features.

**Mod support post-1.0.** API stabilization, Workshop integration.

**Multiplayer deferred to v1.1+.** Per earlier scope decisions.

**Realistic sales targets:** 1,000-10,000 sales first year. 30,000 over the game's lifetime would be strong success.
